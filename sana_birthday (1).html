💌 Dil Ki Baat

Sana, kya bataoon — shayad alfaaz kam pad jayenge... lekin phir bhi koshish karta hoon.

Tum mere liye sirf ek person nahi ho. Tum meri subah ki pehli khushi ho, aur raat ki aakhri dua. Jab bhi dil udaas hota hai, ek teri yaad hi kaafi hoti hai smile lane ke liye.

Teri aankhon mein kuch aisa hai — jab tu mujhe dekhti hai, toh lagta hai sab theek ho jayega. Teri smile... teri smile dekh ke main bhool jaata hoon ke duniya mein koi bhi mushkil hai.

In tasveerons mein tu kitni pyaari lag rahi hai — har ek mein ek alag cheez hai... kabhi hansti hui, kabhi shaan se khadi, kabhi apne blue rang mein chamakti... har baar pyaari.

Aaj tum ek aur saal badi hue — aur main chahta hoon ke yeh saal tumhara sabse khoobsurat ho. Har khwab pura ho. Har din khushi le aaye. Har pal yaadon mein basi jaaye.

Tum deserve karti ho sab kuch — wo pyar, wo sukoon, wo zindagi jo tum imagine karti ho. Aur main chahunga ke hamesha tumhare paas rehun — teri khushiyon mein, teri mushkilon mein, har mod par.

Tumse bahut pyar hai, Sana. Bahut zyada. 💜

Tumhara ZI🤍
